Follow this procedure if you want to change the logo at the top-left of your board.

  1. Upload your logo to your webserver.
  2. Go to Admin CP --> Themes --> Modify / Delete --> Edit your Theme
  3. Change the Forum Logo path to your new logo.

[edit] Alternative method

Alternatively, you can replace the original images/logo.gif with your own logo (but your logo must be in .gif). However, this method may not work with custom themes.
