You should be able to login to the Wiki using the same account information from the MyBB Community Forums.

Please feel to contribute your knowledge of MyBB to existing pages, or in new pages as necessary. The members of the Wiki/Support division of the MyBB Group will be present to assist in maintaining the quality of the content presented in the Wiki. If you haven't edited in a Wiki before, you may wish to use the Sandbox as a test page in order to become familiarized with the functions of a Wiki.

The Wikimedia Meta-Wiki has a good tutorial on how to use "Wikitext" and other Wiki editing tips and tricks and a handbook for MediaWiki operations. See the Editing Help page for more information.

Read the Contribution Standards to ensure that your contributions conform to the quality that is expected on the wiki.

The Todo list lists the tasks that need to be done, and pages that need work.

There are a few Wiki templates that can be used. See MyBB Wiki:Templates for more information.

Each page has its own "discussion" page where you can add your comments to the page. The discussion pages can be used to discuss the content of the Wiki-page that it is associated with. Please refrain from adding support requests on the discussion pages, as the Community Forums would be a more ideal location.

If you find any errors with the Wiki, please, by all means, edit the page to correct it. Otherwise, please contact a Wiki Team member for assistance.
